Crafting a McDonald's Breakfast Burrito

  1. Lay out one tortilla
  2. Split one piece of American cheese in half and lay them length wise on the tortilla
  3. Add 1 3oz scoop of burrito mix to center of tortilla
  4. Roll the burrito by
    1. Fold the left end of the burrito in one inch
    2. Take the bottom edge and fold over the mix pulling it tight
    3. Roll the covered mix towards the top edge
    4. Repeat with burrito wrap around folded tortilla
  5. Place stick on wrap to seal it and voila
